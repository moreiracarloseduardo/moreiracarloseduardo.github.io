About

My professional journey commenced as a FrontEnd Developer at Likin.do. Immersed in the realm of web development, I honed my skills with technologies such as HTML, JavaScript, TypeScript, Angular, and the Ionic Framework. I autonomously steered projects, adeptly integrating front-end solutions with back-end systems, and took a hands-on approach in both creating and updating templates and functionalities. Regular daily meetings and planning sessions not only solidified my role within the team but also fortified my dedication to teamwork and adherence to professional ethics.

Later on, I took a deep dive into the captivating world of gaming at Coruja Games. My role allowed me not just to sharpen my technical skills in Unity3D, C#, and 2D & 3D design, but also to flex my logical thinking, problem-solving, and proactive approach. Under my oversight, games transitioned from the ideation stage right up to launch, with an emphasis on optimizing and ensuring performance.

Fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, I take pride in bringing a multicultural perspective to each project. Beyond my technical skills, I deeply value empathy, creativity, and responsibility. I regard as fundamental in developing solutions that truly resonate with users.

In my portfolio, I invite you to explore a blend of my technical passion and interpersonal commitment. Together, they form the essence of what I bring to the digital world. Dive in!

My Candy Shop

🍭 Candy Craft: Unleash Your Sweet Ambitions 🍬

Concept:

Welcome to Candy Craft, the game where sweet dreams become a reality. Dive into the world of candy creation and business, combining entrepreneurial spirit with a love for confections.

Game Mechanics:

The core mechanics of Pixel Invaders focus on the player's ability to shoot and eliminate oncoming invaders. Players earn both experience points and in-game currency based on the number and type of invaders they defeat. These resources are integral for progression:

  • Ingredient Acquisition: Players can buy a variety of ingredients essential for crafting distinct candies. Availability and cost of ingredients might vary, demanding players to strategize their purchases.
  • Candy Crafting: Combining different ingredients results in varied candy outcomes. Discovering the right mix can unlock rare and high-demand candies.
  • Store Management: Pricing, inventory management, and customer service play pivotal roles in determining store popularity and revenue.
  • Revenue and Upgrades: Selling candies earns money. Accumulated earnings can be invested in store upgrades, unlocking advanced equipment and rare ingredients, thus allowing the creation of more exotic candies.
  • Expansion: As players progress, they have the opportunity to open more stores in different locations, each with its own demand and competition.

Development:

Designed for mobile platforms, Candy Craft is intuitive and engaging, making use of touch controls for a seamless gaming experience. The game offers vibrant graphics and catchy tunes, ensuring players are immersed in their candy ventures.

Conclusion:

Candy Craft is a tribute to everyone's inner child and entrepreneur. Crafting, selling, and expanding, players can delve into the delightful world of candies. I hope this game adds a touch of sweetness to your gaming collection.

Pixel Invaders

🕹️ Pixel Invaders: A Fusion of 3D and Pixel Art 🚀

Concept:

Step into the vibrant universe of Pixel Invaders, where nostalgic pixel art aesthetics harmoniously blend with 3D graphics. The fate of your world rests in your hands as you combat escalating waves of determined invaders.

Game Mechanics:

The core mechanics of Pixel Invaders focus on the player's ability to shoot and eliminate oncoming invaders. Players earn both experience points and in-game currency based on the number and type of invaders they defeat. These resources are integral for progression:

  • Experience Points (XP): Accumulation of XP results in character level-ups, which can potentially unlock new weapons and abilities.
  • In-game Currency: This currency can be expended in the in-game store to purchase weapon upgrades and boosts, each with its unique attributes like firing rate, damage output, and special effects.
  • Movement: The game integrates a swipe mechanic allowing players to dodge enemy projectiles and obstacles. Precision and timing are critical, given that direct contact with invaders results in health depletion or game over.
  • Difficulty Scaling: As players advance to subsequent levels, the speed, resilience, and tactics of the invaders intensify, demanding evolving strategies and constant adaptability.

Development:

Pixel Invaders was meticulously crafted to offer a hybrid experience, combining the retro charm of pixel art with the depth and immersion of 3D graphics. The outcome is a visually unique and captivating game.

Conclusion:

Pixel Invaders invites players to relive the classic days of arcade gaming while indulging in a contemporary presentation. With escalating challenges and addictive gameplay, it's a title that's sure to captivate both gaming veterans and newcomers alike.

Ice Melt Race

🔥 Ice Melt Race: Master the Elements 🌊

Concept:

In Ice Melt Race, players navigate through a frigid environment where the balance between fire and water is crucial. The key lies in utilizing a flame-thrower to overcome icy obstacles, fill up water tanks, and create paths to victory.

Game Mechanics:
  • Fuel Management: Players must ensure their gas tanks are adequately filled. Gas availability and consumption rates require strategic planning to avoid running out during crucial moments.
  • Flame-Thrower: Armed with a flame-thrower, players can target and melt icy barriers. Timing and precision are vital to optimize the melting process without excessive fuel usage.
  • Shader Mechanics: A custom shader was meticulously designed to simulate the realistic melting of ice, providing an immersive visual experience as the ice transitions into water.
  • Water Collection: As the ice melts, the resultant water accumulates in a nearby tank. Players must monitor and manage this level to ensure the tank neither overflows nor remains underfilled.
  • Bridge Operation: With a filled water tank, players can activate mechanisms that raise a bridge, granting access to further game areas. This bridge operation is contingent on the water levels, adding an element of strategy.
  • Objective: The ultimate goal is to safely cross the bridge, navigating challenges and utilizing both fire and water dynamics to emerge victorious.
Development:

Ice Melt Race development was a blend of strategy and visual artistry. The game's standout feature is its custom shader, which simulates ice melting in a captivating manner, enhancing player immersion and underlining the game's core mechanics.

Conclusion:

Ice Melt Race challenges players to think, strategize, and adapt. Through mastering the interplay between fire and water, players can experience the satisfaction of overcoming diverse obstacles and achieving their goals. Engage with the elements and blaze your path to victory.

Run Candy Run!

🍬 Run Candy Run: Dodge, Grow, and Strategize! 🏃

Concept:

Run Candy Run challenges players in a fast-paced world where sweets are your strength and agility is your best ally. Navigate a treacherous path, gather candies to grow, but be wary of sharp obstacles looking to cut you down to size.

Game Mechanics:
  • Runner Dynamics: As with traditional runner games, Run Candy Run emphasizes quick reflexes and forward momentum. The environment continuously evolves, demanding adaptability and foresight from the player.
  • Candy Collection: Scattered throughout the course are candies. Collecting these not only scores points but also causes the player's avatar to grow in size, increasing the risk but providing strategic advantages.
  • Avoidance: Sharp obstacles are the primary adversaries. As the player's body elongates, maneuvering becomes a more intricate dance of risk versus reward, challenging players to dodge impending threats.
  • Strategic Shortcuts: An elongated body isn't just a liability; it can be an asset. Players can leverage their lengthened form to bridge gaps or create shortcuts, turning potential vulnerabilities into game-changing strategies.
  • Endgame Challenge: As the player's body grows and the game progresses, the difficulty amplifies, culminating in an ultimate test of dexterity and decision-making to achieve a cutting end!
Development:

Run Candy Run's development capitalized on the tried-and-true runner mechanics while introducing innovative challenges. The game is designed to be intuitive yet demanding, ensuring players are consistently engaged and aiming for that next high score.

Conclusion:

Run Candy Run is more than just a sprint; it's a strategic marathon of growth, reflexes, and tactics. Every candy collected, every obstacle dodged, and every shortcut created contributes to an experience that's both exhilarating and satisfying. Ready, set, run!

Fridge Master

🍎 Fridge Master: Strategize Your Storage! 🍖

Concept:

Dive into Fridge Master, where the task of organizing groceries becomes an enticing puzzle. Elevate the routine of stocking the fridge into a game of strategic storage and planning.

Game Mechanics:
  • Drag-and-Drop: Reflecting the hands-on approach, players select items by clicking on them and dragging them to their preferred spot in the fridge.
  • Placement Zones: The fridge is divided into zones—main compartment, door storage, and freezer. Every item has a suggested zone, pushing players to maximize the available space.
  • Item-Specific Rules: Dragging an item to the side of the screen sends it to the fridge door, the top puts it in the freezer, and the center goes to the main compartment. These mechanics present a layer of strategy as players deduce the best placement for each item.
  • Challenges: Progress brings more items and variety, ramping up the intricacy of space management. The objective remains: expert organization and optimum storage.
Development:

Fridge Master has been crafted to offer a balance between challenge and intuitiveness. With its real-life-inspired design, players get the satisfaction of a meticulously organized fridge without the actual cleanup.

Conclusion:

Fridge Master is more than a game about storage; it celebrates the art of organization and the satisfaction of space optimization. Whether you're organizationally inclined or a puzzle enthusiast, Fridge Master promises a delightful challenge.
